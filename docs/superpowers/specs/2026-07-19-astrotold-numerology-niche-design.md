# Astrotold numerology-only niche

## Purpose

Narrow Astrotold from general astrology and numerology to a numerology-only Shorts channel, improving audience targeting and topic consistency.

## Content

Scripts will focus on birth dates and numerology numbers, name initials, lucky colours/numbers/days, and timely monthly or weekly love, career, and energy guidance. Zodiac-sign prompts and tags will be removed.

## Safety and delivery

Videos remain English-only, entertainment-focused, and use the existing TTS, captions, upload safety lock, schedule, and isolated channel state.

## Verification

Tests will confirm the niche, prompt direction, seed angles, and default tags exclude zodiac content while preserving the established channel safety settings.
