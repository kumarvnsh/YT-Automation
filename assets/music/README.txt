Drop royalty-free background music here as .mp3 / .m4a / .wav files.
One track is chosen at random per video and ducked under the voiceover
(volume set in config.yaml -> video.background_music.volume).

Free sources:
  - YouTube Audio Library (https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary)  [download as creator]
  - Pixabay Music (https://pixabay.com/music/)
  - Incompetech (https://incompetech.com)  [credit required]

If this folder is empty, videos render fine with voiceover only.
